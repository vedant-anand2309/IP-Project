Recipe 1: Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

This classic Italian pasta dish is creamy, savory, and easy to make. Perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a special occasion.

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Fry pancetta in a pan until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs and Parmesan cheese.
  4. Combine cooked spaghetti with pancetta, then mix in the egg mixture.
  5. Season with salt and black pepper, and serve immediately.

Recipe 2: Chicken Tikka Masala

A flavorful and creamy chicken dish that is a staple in Indian cuisine. This recipe combines marinated chicken with a rich tomato-based sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken breast
  • 200g yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons tikka masala paste
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 400g tomato sauce
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt and tikka masala paste for at least 1 hour.
  2. Cook onions in a pan until golden, then add marinated chicken and cook through.
  3. Stir in tomato sauce and simmer until the sauce thickens.
  4. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with rice or naan bread.
